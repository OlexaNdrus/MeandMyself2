My Bio

Childhood

I was born in Lviv in 1995, although my roots begin in the north of Ukraine. My childhood is the brightest time of my life. Carefree days with many friends playing in a variety of games. There was no limit of our imagination. I used to spend summer in my second hometown - Sumy. It was quite difficult for me to adapt new environment, so every day I was begging parents to go back. It is worth to admit that in my childhood I was an avid collector. Gathering almost everything from stickers to crumbs from bottles of beer.
